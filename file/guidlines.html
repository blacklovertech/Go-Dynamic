Guidelines


  1. Each participant should carry identity card.
  2. The registration fee for KARE students is Rs.100/- per participant.
  3. The registration fee for external students is Rs.200/- per participant.
  4. Participants should be registered on or before 27th April, 2022
  5. There is no restriction for participating in various events unless the timings of two events coincide.
  6. All the events will commence at the scheduled time; Punctuality will be appreciated.
  7. Lunch will be provided.
  8. The decision of judges will be final.
  9. Violation of rules & regulations are prohibited.
  10. Discipline is mandatory inside the Campus.
